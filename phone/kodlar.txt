csv olarak çekme kodu:

Get-ADUser -Filter * -Properties Mobile,mail,msExchHideFromAddressLists,DisplayName,userAccountControl |
Select-Object DisplayName,mail,Mobile,msExchHideFromAddressLists,userAccountControl |
Export-Csv -Path "ADUserExport.csv" -NoTypeInformation -Encoding UTF8


json olarak çekme kodu:

Get-ADUser -Filter * -Properties Mobile,mail,msExchHideFromAddressLists,DisplayName,userAccountControl |
Select-Object DisplayName,mail,Mobile,msExchHideFromAddressLists,userAccountControl |
ConvertTo-Json -Depth 3 | Out-File -FilePath "ADUserExport.json" -Encoding UTF8
